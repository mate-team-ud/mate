Child Safety Standards

Mate is committed to the prevention of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We enforce strict age requirements, moderate content, and provide in-app reporting tools. Learn more in our full policy below.

Child Safety Policy

Prevention: We require all users to be at least 18 years old. Age is verified, and any attempts to create accounts with false information will result in account removal.

Moderation: User content is automatically and manually reviewed to detect and remove harmful or inappropriate material.

Reporting: Users can easily report inappropriate content or behavior. All reports are reviewed and escalated when necessary.

Cooperation with Authorities: We report all confirmed cases of CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material) to regional and national authorities in accordance with the law.
